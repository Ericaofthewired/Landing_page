Meet the gym trainers!

Brock

Leader of the Pewter City gym, Brock specializes in Rock-type pokemon. His gym is located in the northern part of Kanto, near the mountains.

Misty

Leader of the Cerulean City gym, Misty specializes in Water-type pokemon. Her gym is located in the seaside town of Cerulean City.

Lt. Surge

Leader of the Vermilion City gym, Lt. Surge specializes in Electric-type pokemon. His gym is located in the east-central part of Kanto, near the ocean.

Erika

Leader of the Celadon City gym, Erika specializes in Grass-type pokemon. Her gym is located in the lush and green city of Celadon.

Koga

Leader of the Fuchsia City gym, Koga specializes in Poison-type pokemon. His gym is located in the southern part of Kanto, near the Safari Zone.

Sabrina

Leader of the Saffron City gym, Sabrina specializes in Psychic-type pokemon. Her gym is located in the bustling city of Saffron.

Blaine

Leader of the Cinnabar Island gym, Blaine specializes in Fire-type pokemon. His gym is located on the volcanic island of Cinnabar.

Giovanni

Leader of the Viridian City gym, Giovanni specializes in Ground-type pokemon. His gym is located in the central part of Kanto, near the Viridian Forest.
